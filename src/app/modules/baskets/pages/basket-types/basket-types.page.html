Baskets on Ethereum Mainnet support the best known DeFi protocols. We are working towards deploying on other blockchains where gas fees are lower and acceptable for smaller budget allocations.
DeFi Basket
This basket comprises of Uniswap, Balancer and Mooniswap pools as well as ERC-20 tokens, which are selected as the highest yielding baskets. You can use our pre-selected pools and ETH allocation in them, or you can customize it yourself, using the pools/coins you want and choose in which pool or coin you want to invest the most ETH. You can also tell our algorithm the level of Risk you want to take. This will make the return higher, but might make the loss higher as well.
